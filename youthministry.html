Youth Ministry

Young People what they have plenty is time and energy, and with this God Given resources use them for service in the house of the Lord. The St.Paul youth is a mixture of teens and young adults. what they have is a passion for God in every way. Participating in circuit rallies, leading Praise and Worship in Church. The young People in church have a lot to offer in terms of talent and Skill. Here at St.paul we have seen talents grow and blossom and even now we like to nuture and embrace our talents around us and use them to serve in the house of the Lord. in the Bible Paul talks to timothy about being a young person in the world and he tells him, "Do not let anyone despise you because of your youth, but set an example to believers in SPEECH, CONDUCT, LOVE, FAITH and PURITY"
