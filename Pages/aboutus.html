Summary of Methodist Doctrines

The Methodist Church claims and cherishes its place in the Holy Catholic Church, which is the Body of Christ.

The Doctrines of the Evangelical Faith, the Methodist Church acknowledges this revelation as the supreme rule of faith and practice.

Conference is the final authority within the Church with regard to its doctrines and all questions concerning the interpretation of doctrines.

Christ's Ministers in the Church are stewards in the household of God, and shepherds of His flock.

It is the universal conviction of the Methodist people that the office of the Christian Ministry depends upon the call of God.

The Preachers, itinerant and lay are examined tested and approved before they are authorised to minister in holy things.

Objectives of the Church

To empower believers into the faith and freedom through God's Word.

To promote social holiness through the spirit of Methodism.

To inspire and mentor excellence in Church leadership.

To enhance continued membership growth and Church sustainability.
